About Uncle Chipps

Uncle Chipps is one of India’s most beloved snack brands, known for its light, crispy texture and unforgettable flavor.

Launched in the 1990s, it quickly became a household name with the jingle: “Bole mere lips, I love Uncle Chipps!”

More than just a chip, Uncle Chipps is a part of family fun, nostalgia, and unforgettable memories shared over a packet full of happiness.
